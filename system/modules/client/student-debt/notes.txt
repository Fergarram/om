NAME
       debtclicker — incremental clicker about paying debt by subtraction

SYNOPSIS
       You start with a huge debt.
       Clicking sells items.
       Time advances.
       If you fail to pay the minimum, things get worse.

TIME SCALE
       1 day        = 3 seconds
       1 month      = 90 seconds
       1 year       ≈ 18.25 minutes
       20 years     ≈ 6 hours

CORE VARIABLES
       debt         Large negative number (e.g. -300k to -500k)
       cash         Liquid money
       bank         Locked savings used for upgrades
       stress       Risk multiplier
       month        Advances every 90s

CORE LOOP
       Click        → sell goods → +$1 per click (base)
       Month tick   → interest added to debt
                   → minimum payment checked
                   → stress + unlucky events rolled

CLICKING
       ~1000 clicks per minute max
       ≈ $1,500 per month from pure clicking
       Clicking alone can pay debt in theory

MONTHLY MINIMUM
       Set slightly below max click income:
           $900–$1,200 early game
       Missing minimum:
           tax penalty
           stress increase

BANK
       Cash moved to bank:
           cannot pay debt
           unlocks upgrades
       Protects money from robbery

UPGRADES
       better candy              +click value
       home-made cookies         +click value, monthly cost
       magic cookies             big +click, +stress, legal risk
       t-shirts                  passive income
       little brother seller     salary OR commission
       vending machine           repeatable passive income,
                                 increases monthly obligations

STRESS
       Increases event chance and severity
       Reduces click efficiency
       Never blocks clicking completely

UNLUCKY EVENTS (MONTHLY)
       caught selling magic cookies
           fine, income disabled, stress spike
       robbed
           lose % of cash (bank safe)
       car accident
           cash loss or forced monthly expense
       getting sick
           reduced or disabled clicking for N months

DESIGN CONSTRAINTS
       Clicking must always work
       No hard softlocks
       Progress is subtraction only
       Zero debt is not the end

END
       The player doesn’t escape the system.
       They only manage it better.
